## Quick Reference (back of card)

**Roll:** 2d6 + mods

| Result | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| 1-5 | Fail |
| 6-8 | Success, but... |
| 9-10 | Success |
| 11+ | Success, and... |

Snake eyes = "but" at best
Boxcars = "and" minimum
